Imagine Dragons

The Imagine Dragons was formed on 2008. based in Las Vegas, Nevada. The genre of music of what Imagine Dragon play is: pop rock, electropop, indie pop, indie rock, arena rock, and alternative rock. Their most popular album is Mercury, where is divided in 2 parts, best know as act 1 & 2.
The band is the recipient of a Grammy Award, three American Music Awards, ten Billboard Music Award, eight BMI pop Musica Awards, an Echo Award, a MTV Video Music Award, five Teen Choice Awards and a World Music Award. They also make songs from other things that they like, for example, a collaboration with the videogame League of Legends where they made song called "enemy" that it was more directly to the TV serie than the game.
